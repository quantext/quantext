Beta signup

Below are details of the beta signup for Quantext

There are 2 ways to experience Quantext, depending on your needs.

The free tier is the default tier for new signups. There are no costs associated with this tier (and never will be!)... perfect for trying out the core functionality of Quantext. This tier is has some limited functionality, but the good news is you can upgrade your plan at any time!

The beta signup tier is the first of our paid plans. It comes with advanced metrics, and some additional functionality such as basic reporting and data export options. Please email info@quantext.co.nz to sign up to the beta.
