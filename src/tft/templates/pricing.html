Pricing

Below are the pricing plans for Quantext

There are 5 Quantext tiers to choose from, depending on your needs.

The free tier is the default tier for new signups. There are no costs associated with this tier (and never will be!)... perfect for trying out the core functionality of Quantext. This tier is has some limited functionality, but the good news is you can upgrade your plan at any time!

The egg tier is the first of our paid plans. It comes with advanced metrics, and some additional functionality such as basic reporting and data export options.

For those who want to explore even more, the hatchling tier offers a labelling interface for automatically classifying response data, as well as some advanced reporting features.

For those who really want to spread their wings, our full flight tier offers an impressive array of functionality and some unique interfaces such as Quantext Live and the student dashboard. We also offer custom modelling services to full flighters (at a negotiable extra charge).

Finally, institutions may want to consider signing up to the flock tier - unlimited full flight licenses for your staff (excellent savings for institutions with more than 10 users!)
